Cheesy Chicken!

A piece of fried chicken as cheesy as you can get it! It goes great with pasta, or as the perfect entree.

Ingredients

  • "Cleaned" chicken breast
  • 2 eggs
  • Seasoned flour
  • Buttermilk
  • Cornflakes
  • Cream cheese
  • Your favorite kind of cheese
  • Optinal: A nice bed of pasta!

Tools

  • Frying mechanism. Be it a pan with oil or a fryer. A fryer works best.
  • Oven set to 350-400 degrees.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven, and turn on fryer to 350 degrees.
  2. mix both eggs and a quarter cup of buttermilk together,to an even consistency throughout.
  3. set up a breading station with 3 bowls of ingredients in this order. Flour. Egg Buttermilk mix. Cornflakes.
  4. Coat chicken in flour.
  5. Coat floured chicken in egg buttermilk mix, then immediately cornflakes.
  6. Make sure its evenly and throughly coated
  7. Put into the fryer. Note that cook times will vary depending on how big the chicken breast is, and how much coating was put on.
  8. To ensure proper cooking, cut into the breast. If there is any sign it is undercooked, it needs to be fried for longer.
  9. When the chicken is cooked throughly. Spread a layer of cream cheese over the top. Put as much as you want!
  10. Put your favorite cheese on top! Again as much as you want.
  11. Put the cooked and cheesed chicken in the over. Cook times will vary based on how much cheese was put on.
  12. When the cheese is bubbly and brown on top take it out.
  13. Make sure the oven and fryer are turned off!
  14. Serve over a bed of your favorite pasta, or with anything.
  15. Enjoy!
