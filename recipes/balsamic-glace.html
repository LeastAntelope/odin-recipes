Balsamic Glace

Balsamic Glace is amazing on steaks, or chicken, or anything else really. It has a unique sweet, yet surprisingly tangy taste, and aroma.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 cup Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Mix all of both ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat. You will want to stir the mixture throughout the process.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat.
  3. Simmer the mixture till it thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon.
  4. Let the finished product cool and serve. Does not need to be refrigerated.
  5. Enjoy!
